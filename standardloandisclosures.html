Borrower's Authorization Form

This Disclosure Serves As An Authorization Form For Submitting A Loan To A Lender. Furthermore, It Authorizes An Agency To Pull Credit, And Contact Employers For Income Verification, And Lenders For Asset Verification. Document Borrower's Authorization Form

Fair Lending Notice

This Disclosure Provides Contact Information For Complaints Based On Discriminatory Practices By Lenders.
Document Fair Lending Notice

Equal Credit Opportunity Act

This Disclosure Notifies The Applicant That It Is Unlawful For Any Creditor To Discriminate Under The Basis Of Race, Color, Religion, National Origin, Sex, Marital Status, Or Age. Document Equal Credit Opportunity Act

Request For Transcript Of Tax Return

This Disclosure Notifes The Applicant That The Lender May Request A Transcript Of Your Tax Returns From The IRS To Ensure That The Income Provided Was Not Fraudulent. Please TYPE Section #1a, #1b, #2a, #2b, #3 Prior To Printing, Signing & Dating Document. Document 4506-T

Right To Receive Apprasail Disclosure

This Disclosures Notifies The Applicant Of The Right To Receive A Copy Of The Appraisal Report. And That The Appraisal Would Be The ONLY Applicant's Upfront Cost. Document Right To Receive Appraisal Report
